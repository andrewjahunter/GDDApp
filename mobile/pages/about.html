Godochurch was build originally as a simple communication tool for a specific pastorate in the His People Baxter church. Other pastors from the area wanted to try it out and it worked. Word of mouth spread, more and more features were added and new modules were developed and as they say, the rest is history....
The guys working on the system are all pastors and leaders in various churches, and have a passion to help churches in their administration, communication and being faithfull shepherds in the kingdom of God
