      No Active Churches

      There are currently no active churches registered in your profile. This is most likely because you have just signed in for the first time.

      TO START: you need to activate at least one church in your profile. To find the church you are looking for click the button below.

      Connect New Church

      Search...

      Enter the name of the church you are looking for. You can enter part of the name for example "His" to search for all "His People" churches

      From the results, click the "check" icon. This will activate the church in your profile

      The church will now appear in the list of available database to log into. Simply click on church name to sign in

      Find Church

      This app can be signed into any number of churches/ministries as you would like

      The list below includes all the churches that are active in your profile. Simply click on the church name to be logged into that database

      To remove a church from your list of active profiles simply click the "X" next to the churches name. Note this will simply remove it from your active profile. You can add it back at any stage.

      If there are no churches in the list below or if you want to add another church to your acive list, click "Connect to New Church" under the actions menu

      Missing?

      If you are not able to find the church you are looking for, its most likely because they are not registered as a church on the godochurch online systems. If this is the case please choose from the following options:

      1. Please ask your church to consider registering with us. Send them to our website at www.gododata.com
      2. Send us an email at support@gododata.com, and we will contact them for you.
      thanks!
