Location Info

A group must have valid location information.

Valid location information makes it much easier for people to locate small groups that meet their needs. It also provides us with useful information about where people are being shepherded in their communities.

We need at least a valid latitiude and longitude position which you can obtain by either clicking on the map provided or typing the address into the text box.

You do not need to provide an exact location. You can edit/delete the street address and number if you prefer.
